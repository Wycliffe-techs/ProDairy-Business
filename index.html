About Us

Prodairy EA Ltd assists farmers with master-plans, well designed, modern, efficient cow housing, farm buildings and structures based on the latest research and technology. We are the dealers of state of the art cow barn equipment (https://www.agriprom.nl) and herd management systems which guarantees cow and working comfort. Currently mainly headlocks and cubicles.
